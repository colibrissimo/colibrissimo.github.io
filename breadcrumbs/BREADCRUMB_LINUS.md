## WHAT I BUILT

**CYE Physics Engine — "Feel the Tense" v0.1**

Matter.js physics, single HTML file. Three tenses of the same sentence fall with gravity. Student drags them to timeline zones.

Each tense has different physics:
- **Past simple:** dense (0.0032), low bounce (0.3), high air friction. Drops like a stone. It's over.
- **Present perfect:** medium (0.0020), balanced bounce (0.5). Has weight but still moving. The bridge.
- **Present simple:** light (0.0010), bouncy (0.7), low friction. Quick, alive, routine.

Physics is texture. Texture teaches. Weight is meaning.

Features: collision sounds, correct/wrong audio feedback, Zero-G toggle, Explode button as reward, Stopwatch timer, 10 sentences, British English, GEII context.

File: `cye_physics_v01.html` — standalone, no dependencies except Matter.js CDN.
